Strategy 1: Moving Average Crossover

A mathematical trend strategy. Calculates the arithmetic mean of closing prices over a short period and compares it to a long period to trigger signals.

Strategy 2: RSI Mean Reversion

Uses the Relative Strength Index formula to measure the magnitude of recent price changes, mathematically identifying overbought (RSI > 70) and oversold (RSI < 30) assets.

RSI Mean Reversion

Strategy 3: MACD Momentum

A pure mathematical oscillator. It subtracts a 26-period Exponential Moving Average (EMA) from a 12-period EMA to calculate market momentum and trend reversals.

MACD Momentum

Strategy 4: Bollinger Bands Volatility

Bollinger Bands Volatility

The Volume Weighted Average Price calculates the ratio of an asset's cumulative value traded to its cumulative volume traded over a session to find the true mathematical average price.
